My Education Journey

★education is the movement from darkness to light ★

I attended Tadika Kemas PPK Pauh 2 for 3 years. I successfully completed preschool in 2009 at the age of 6.
Subsequently, I continued my studies at Sekolah Kebangsaan Pauh until I finished sixth grade and
obtained 3A 2B in UPSR. For my secondary education, I attended a cluster school of excellence,
Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Dato' Sheikh Ahmad, from Form 1 to Form 5,
and successfully achieved 5A in SPM.

After completing my schooling, I decided to continue my education at the diploma level
by enrolling in Pre-University (Form 6) at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Syed Sirajudin,
where I achieved impressive STPM results. Now, I have successfully continued my studies
at the bachelor's degree level at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Merbok, Kedah,
majoring in Library Management.
